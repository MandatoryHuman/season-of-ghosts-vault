## Bio

Otonashi Shio is the twin brother of [[Otonashi Amaya|Amaya]] and the son of [[Otonashi Nagi|Nagi]] and [[Otonashi Kairi|Kairi]]. Born shortly after his parents traded the relentless city of [[Sze]] for the quiet mists of [[Willowshore]], Shio grew up steeped in his family's deep connection to the water. He is a man of profound patience, steady habits, and a grounded, unyielding strength.

While his mother made her living plunging into the perilous depths of the [[Sea of Ghosts]], Shio found his calling gliding across the moonlit surface of Willowshore's rivers. Working as an [ukai fisher](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cormorant_fishing), Shio spends his nights on a silent, flat-bottomed skiff, guiding his flock of trained cormorants by the warm glow of a suspended fire basket. It is an atmospheric, traditional craft that requires a deep bond with his birds and a practised hand to harvest the river's bounty.

Beyond his nocturnal work on the water, Shio is a person of meticulous routine. In his spare time during the daylight hours, he cultivates delicate bonsai trees in his garden. He is a familiar, comforting presence in Willowshore; the locals joke that they can set their clocks by the sight of his lantern returning to the docks just as the morning mist begins to lift.
