This grove of Sugi trees is named after a famed warrior from years past. The grove was discovered by in 7020 IC by the monk [[Zhi Hu]] when the [[Kami]] that lives within [[The Great Willow]] revealed the location to her. She would then found the [[Tan Sugi Monastery]] in the same location.
