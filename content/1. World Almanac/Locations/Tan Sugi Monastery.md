## Description

The Tan Sugi Monastery was founded in 7020 IC by the monk [[Zhi Hui]]. Its construction led to the creation of [[Willowshore]] as a town to support it, though since the monastery's fall in 7060, Willowshore has been more focused on its lumber industry.

Zhi Hu founded the monastery when the [[Kami]] that lives within [[The Great Willow]] revealed the location of a [[Tan Sugi Grove]] to the West. The site soon became a popular destination for pilgrimage, and a monastery was built to make it a more permanent fixture.

After Zhi Hu's death in 7054, the monastery fell into disrepair, and by 7060 it was entirely abandoned. A few years later, in 7062, a group of woodcutters from Willowshore visited the site to attempt to cut the ancient [sugi trees](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cryptomeria) growing around the now-ruin, but they instead returned to town as undead creatures, their weapons shattered. This became known as the "[[Night of Broken Blades]]".
