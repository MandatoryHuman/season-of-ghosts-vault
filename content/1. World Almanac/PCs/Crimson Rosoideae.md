## Backstory

Crimson was born and raised in Willowshore as a child of the Leshy Saloon but found that there was nowhere worthy of developing their magical talents. Because of this, Crimson left town to study in a distant Magic Academy studying Unified Magical Theory, the night before their final exam Crimson pushed themselves to pull an all-nighter, mere hours before the exam however they passed out and reformed into a solitary rose. For two years Crimson was handed from lover to lover as a novelty. A rose that never wilts. Then three weeks ago they awoke once more. Crimson has come to Willowshore to study under the renowned Anjal, unfortunately with his recent passing Crimson has had to settle for studying under Anjal's apprentice.
