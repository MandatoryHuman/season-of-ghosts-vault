## Overview

![[z_assets/Creatures/Ghost Types/Hungry Ghost.webp|float-left|400]]These spirits are a type of Yóu Hún Yě Guǐ, or "wandering ghost". They represent those who lived in extreme greed and refused to help others. They are cursed with insatiable appetites, bloated bellies, and thin necks. Food they attempt to eat bursts into flames or turns to ash.

## Behaviour and Haunts

-

### Recognition Guide

- These spirits are best recognised by their appearance; their large bellies and thin necks.

### Associated Hazards

- Theft of food.

## Exorcism and Resolution

- Offering food to the ghost can satiate it temporarily. The food must be accompanied by a special chant or ritual to actually allow the ghost to consume it.
- Burning of "Hell Money" or Joss paper can calm them.
- Monks and priests can chant and pray for the ghosts. The good karma generated from these chants can allow the ghost to move on.
- Some communities put on theatrical shows, leaving the front row empty in hopes that the ghosts will enjoy the entertainment and be satisfied.
