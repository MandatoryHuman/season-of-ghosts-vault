## Overview

![[z_assets/Creatures/Ghost Types/Maternal Ghost.webp|float-left|300]]The spirit of a woman who died in childbirth or leaving a new-born behind. She is not typically malicious, but are still dangerous.

## Behaviour and Haunts

- Ubume will appear on dark roads, crying out for someone to hold her baby. If a traveller takes the child, it becomes heavier and heavier until it crushes them to death

## Exorcism and Resolution

- If one is strong enough to accept the Ubume's child and survive, it can aid the ghost in passing on to the other side.
