## Overview

Phi Tai Hong are spirits of those who died sudden and violent deaths. This means that they did not have a chance to prepare themselves for death, dooming them to return as a ghost.

## Behaviour and Haunts

- The sudden nature of a Phi Tai Hong's death means that the spirit was unable to fulfil their dreams and desires while alive. This means that they are very unpredictable and dangerous, especially for the first few days or weeks when they are likely to be seeking revenge.

### Associated Hazards

- Death in the same manner the ghost suffered.

## Exorcism and Resolution

- Because these spirits actively haunt the area it died, untrained people are advised to stay clear. Only the best trained priests should approach to perform exorcisms.
