## Bio

The founder of the [[Tan Sugi Monastery]], who's construction led to the creation of [[Willowshore]] as a town to support it. Zhi Hu founded the monastery when the [[Kami]] that lives within [[The Great Willow]] revealed the location of a [[Tan Sugi Grove]] to the West. The site soon became a popular destination for pilgrimage, and a monastery was built to make it a more permanent fixture.

After Zhi Hu's death in 7054, the monastery fell into disrepair, and by 7060 it was entirely abandoned.
